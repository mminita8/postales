This project focuses on the postal operators in Ecuador and employs public information from the Ministry of Telecom, and the Superintendency of companies.

The *.txt are the results as follows:

* bal2020.txt - the financial results of the Ecuadorian Companies reported at SUPERCIAS.  This archive is available in https://reporteria.supercias.gob.ec/portal/cgi-bin/cognos.cgi?b_action=cognosViewer&ui.action=run&ui.object=%2fcontent%2ffolder%5b%40name%3d%27Reportes%27%5d%2ffolder%5b%40name%3d%27Estados%20Financieros%27%5d%2freport%5b%40name%3d%27Estados%20Financieros%20x%20Rama%27%5d&ui.name=Estados%20Financieros%20x%20Rama&run.outputFormat=&run.prompt=true

* EEFFTH.txt - contains the financial results of the companies with postal authorization that present information to SUPERCIAS (results of applying script.R).

* posibles.txt - contains the financial results of possible companies that could need postal authorization for legally working in the country (results of applying script.R).

* posiblesH53.txt - financial results of companies with H53 as International Standard Industrial Classification, which must have postal autorization for legally working in the country (results of applying script.R).

* registrados.xlsx - list of companies and persons with postal autorization for legally working in the country.
